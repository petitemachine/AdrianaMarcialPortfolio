Adriana Marcial



I'm an ambitious, curious, and enthusiastic professional who thrives on learning and connecting with people. Seven years ago, I made a bold choice: to pause my career, immerse myself in a new language, and experience the world from the inside — living in four countries and visiting over 25. That decision transformed me. I returned with a culturally rich mindset, a high capacity for empathy, and a practical toolkit built in fast-paced, high-pressure environments — including strong communication, delegation, critical thinking, and a keen eye for detail. Fluent in two languages, I've always found it natural to work in diverse, multicultural spaces. Since the birth of my child, I've kept my skills current through focused online learning and leadership activities. Now, I'm ready to return to the digital industry — bringing confidence, resilience, and a genuine love for the work.
